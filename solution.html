§3 The Solution

A 3D-printed, 4-wheeled UGV with onboard Raspberry Pi and camera

Specifications

Length177.8 mm (7")
Width88.9 mm (3.5")
Wall height31.75 mm (1.25")
Chassis3D-printed PLA, open-top rectangular tub
ComputeRaspberry Pi (Raspberry Pi OS)
Drive2 × low-cost DC motors via motor driver
PowerDual battery pack
SensorOnboard camera module (live video)

Bill of materials

ComponentCost
Raspberry Pi~$35
Motor driver~$5
Dual battery pack~$10
2 × DC motors~$10
3D-printed PLA chassis~$3 (filament)
Total per unit~$63

Why ground, not air?

An aerial alternative was considered but rejected after a Weighted Benefit Analysis (see §5 — Evidence). Ground-based is quieter, lower-profile, and ~70% cheaper to build, which matters more for a unit intended to be expendable in tight indoor environments.
